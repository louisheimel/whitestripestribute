Facts

  • The White Stripes formed in 1997 in Detroit, Michigan
  • The band consisted of two members: Jack White who was a songwriter, sang vocals, played guitar, piano, and mandolin, and Meg White who played the drums and sang vocals.
  • Meg's minimalistic drumming style is a key part of the band's sound.
  • The band is heavily influenced by blues rock bands such as the Rolling Stones, AC/DC, and Led Zeppelin.
